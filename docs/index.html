Welcome!

Welcome to Anthology of Computers and the Humanities. The Anthology offers an open-access, digital archive that hosts peer-reviewed conference papers from digital humanities conferences and workshops. The archive is indexed across the most popular scholarly indices and include support for persistent identifiers including DOIs (digital object identifier) and ORCID iDs. Check out the first set of volumes below. And please get in touch in you are interested in hosting your conference proceedings with us!
