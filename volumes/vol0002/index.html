Anthology of Computers and the Humanities

Volume 2, October 2025

Digital Humanities Tech Symposium 2025

Edited by Julia Damerow and Rebecca Sutton Koeser

The proceedings of the Digital Humanities Tech Symposium, held as part of DH2025 at Nova University in Lisbon, Portugal (July 14–18, 2025), showcase contributions that foreground the technical dimensions of digital humanities work.

  • Proceedings of Digital Humanities Tech Symposium 2025: Introduction
    Rebecca Sutton Koeser, Jose Angel Hernandez, Julia Damerow, Malte Vogl, Robert Casties. 1–5.
  • There and back again—how to preserve your data during transformations
    Robert Casties. 6–12.
  • Developing Training and Education Resources for Research Software Engineering in DH
    Julia Damerow, David Ragnar Nelson, Jose Hernandez. 13–18.
  • Extending Recogito Studio with Plugins
    Jamie Folsom. 19–24.
  • Historical data visual exploration meets static web technologies
    Paul Girard. 25–31.
  • Clouds for Crowds—Implementing Federated AAI for the Digital Humanities
    Tibor Kálmán, Sally Chambers, Peter Gietz. 32–37.
  • Undate in Action
    Rebecca Sutton Koeser. 38–53.
  • One Template to Rule Them All: Interactive Research Data Documentation with Quarto
    Moritz Mähr, Moritz Twente. 54–67.
